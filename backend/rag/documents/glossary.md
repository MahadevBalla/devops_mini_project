# India Personal Finance Glossary

**AUM**: Assets Under Management — total market value of funds managed by an AMC.
**CAGR**: Compound Annual Growth Rate — growth rate of lump-sum investment over time.
**XIRR**: Extended Internal Rate of Return — annualised return for irregular cashflows like SIPs.
**SIP**: Systematic Investment Plan — fixed amount invested in mutual fund at regular intervals.
**SWR**: Safe Withdrawal Rate — % of corpus withdrawn annually in retirement without depleting corpus.
**TER**: Total Expense Ratio — annual fee charged by mutual fund as % of AUM. Directly reduces returns.
**NAV**: Net Asset Value — price per unit of a mutual fund. NAV = (assets - liabilities) / units.
**ISIN**: International Securities Identification Number — 12-character unique identifier for securities.
**ELSS**: Equity Linked Savings Scheme — tax-saving mutual fund with 3-year lock-in under 80C.
**PPF**: Public Provident Fund — government-backed savings scheme, 15-year lock-in, 7.1% interest.
**EPF**: Employee Provident Fund — mandatory retirement savings for salaried employees.
**NPS**: National Pension System — PFRDA-regulated retirement account, market-linked, locked till 60.
**PRAN**: Permanent Retirement Account Number — unique NPS account number, portable across employers.
**UAN**: Universal Account Number — unique EPF account number, portable across employers.
**HRA**: House Rent Allowance — salary component, partially exempt from tax under old regime.
**LTA**: Leave Travel Allowance — travel expense reimbursement, exempt from tax twice in 4-year block.
**TDS**: Tax Deducted at Source — tax withheld by payer before payment (employer, bank, etc.).
**LTCG**: Long-Term Capital Gains — profit from selling asset held > 12 months (equity) or > 24 months (debt).
**STCG**: Short-Term Capital Gains — profit from selling asset within 12 months (equity) or 24 months (debt).
**Alpha**: Mutual fund return above benchmark. Positive alpha indicates fund manager skill.
**Beta**: Sensitivity of fund to market movements. Beta > 1 = more volatile than market.
**Sharpe Ratio**: Excess return per unit of risk. Higher Sharpe = better risk-adjusted performance.
**AMC**: Asset Management Company — entity that manages mutual funds (HDFC AMC, SBI MF, etc.).
**AMFI**: Association of Mutual Funds in India — industry body, publishes daily NAV for all schemes.
**SEBI**: Securities and Exchange Board of India — regulator for capital markets and mutual funds.
**IRDAI**: Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India — regulator for insurance companies.
**PFRDA**: Pension Fund Regulatory and Development Authority — regulator for NPS.
**RBI**: Reserve Bank of India — central bank, regulates banks and monetary policy.
**Repo Rate**: Rate at which RBI lends to commercial banks. Affects home loan and FD rates.
**Inflation**: Rise in general price level. India CPI inflation target: 4% (RBI mandate).
**STP**: Systematic Transfer Plan — auto-transfer from one fund to another at intervals (e.g., liquid to equity).
**SWP**: Systematic Withdrawal Plan — auto-redemption from fund at regular intervals. Used in retirement.
**Rebalancing**: Restoring target asset allocation (e.g., 60:40 equity:debt) when market moves shift it.
**Corpus**: Accumulated investment pool. "FIRE corpus" = total savings needed for financial independence.
**Folio**: Account number with a mutual fund AMC. One investor can have multiple folios.
**Direct Plan**: Mutual fund bought without distributor. Lower TER than regular plan.
**Regular Plan**: Mutual fund bought through distributor/advisor. Higher TER (commission included).
